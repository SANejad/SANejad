# Hi, I'm Sal — Space Plasma Physicist

I study wave–plasma interactions in the near-Earth space environment, with a focus on how structured ionospheric and magnetospheric plasmas control wave propagation, and particle dynamics, and space whether.

## Research
- Ionosphere–magnetosphere coupling
- Wave–particle interactions
- Radiation belt dynamics

## Methods
- Theoretical and computational modeling
- Active ionospheric experiments
- Spacecraft and ground-based observations
- Data analysis
- AI-enabled tools for space physics

### ELF–VLF Whistler-Mode Wave Ducting and Wave–Particle Interaction

An investigation of how plasma-density and magnetic-field structures guide ELF/VLF whistler-mode waves and modify their interaction with energetic electrons. The project examines wave localization, propagation pathways, resonance conditions, and the role of ducting in particle acceleration, scattering, and precipitation.
